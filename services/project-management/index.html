Project Management

Professional project management for renovation and reinstatement.

Sail Renovate provides dedicated project management to co-ordinate surveys, trades, and delivery for complex renovation projects. We ensure your project stays on time, within budget, and meets quality standards from start to finish.

What we do

Project management definition and scope.

Full Project Co-ordination

A dedicated project manager oversees all aspects of your renovation, from initial planning through to final completion and handover.

Trade Co-ordination

Manage multiple trades and contractors, ensuring a seamless workflow and preventing costly delays between different stages of work.

Quality Assurance

Regular site inspections, progress monitoring, and stringent quality control to ensure work meets agreed standards throughout.

Client Communication

Regular progress updates, clear reporting, and a dedicated point of contact to keep you informed and involved every step of the way.

Our Process

Structured project management process overview.

1

Planning & Preparation

Initial consultation, site survey, detailed planning, and programme development with clear milestones.

2

Trade Co-ordination

Contractor selection, scheduling, and on-site management to ensure progression through each phase.

3

Quality Monitoring

Regular inspections, progress tracking, and quality control to maintain standards throughout the lifecycle.

4

Completion & Handover

Final inspections, client walkthrough, detailed documentation, and a comprehensive handover.
