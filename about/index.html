About Us

Bristol's trusted team for quality renovations.

We are a surveyor-led renovation and reinstatement company, dedicated to bringing professionalism, transparency, and exceptional craftsmanship to every project.

Our Story

Built on a foundation of trust and expertise.

Sail Renovate was founded with a clear mission: to elevate the standard of property renovations and insurance reinstatements in Bristol and the surrounding areas.

With over a decade of experience across the construction and insurance sectors, our founders recognised that homeowners needed more than just skilled tradespeople — they needed reliable project management, clear communication, and the reassurance of qualified oversight.

Today, we handle everything from emergency insurance repairs to full home transformations, all managed by our dedicated team of qualified surveyors and experienced project managers.

The Sail Difference

Our core values.

Surveyor-Led Delivery

Every project is assessed, scoped, and overseen by a qualified surveyor. This ensures accuracy in our quoting and adherence to the highest building standards throughout the build.

Transparent Communication

We believe in keeping you informed. You'll have a dedicated point of contact providing regular updates, so you always know exactly what is happening with your property.

Exceptional Craftsmanship

We never compromise on quality. Our network of vetted, certified tradespeople take immense pride in their work, resulting in finishes that stand the test of time.

Trusted by homeowners and insurers alike.

Our rigorous standards have earned us the trust of major insurance providers to handle complex reinstatement works. We bring that exact same level of scrutiny, project management, and attention to detail to our private home renovations.
