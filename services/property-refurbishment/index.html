Property Refurbishment

Full home refurbishment for kitchens, bathrooms and living spaces.

Sail Renovate transforms properties with coherent design, skilled trade delivery and a secure programme. We refurbish individual rooms and whole homes with the same attention to detail.

What we do

Types of refurbishment work undertaken.

Kitchen Renovations

Complete kitchen makeovers including cabinetry, worktops, appliances, lighting, and flooring. We handle everything from small updates to full gut renovations.

Bathroom Refurbishments

Bathroom transformations from simple refreshes to luxury spa-like spaces. Includes tiling, plumbing, fixtures, ventilation, and accessibility improvements.

Full Property Renovations

Whole-home refurbishments that modernise living spaces, improve energy efficiency, and increase property value through coordinated design and quality finishes.

Living Space Updates

Dining rooms, living rooms, hallways, and bedrooms refreshed with new flooring, lighting, paint, and decorative finishes to create modern, welcoming spaces.
