Claims Management

Professional claims management for insurance reinstatement.

Sail Renovate specialises in managing insurance claims from assessment through to completion. We liaise with insurers, surveyors, and homeowners to ensure smooth reinstatement processes and optimal claim outcomes.

What we do

Claims management explanation and process.

Claim Assessment & Scoping

Initial evaluation of damage, co-ordination with loss adjusters, and development of detailed reinstatement scopes that meet insurance requirements exactly.

Insurer Liaison

Dedicated communication with insurance companies, regular progress reporting, and streamlined management of approvals throughout the project lifecycle.

Contractor Co-ordination

Selection and management of qualified trades, intelligent scheduling to minimise disruption, and strict quality control.

Documentation & Completion

Comprehensive record-keeping, final inspections, and thorough handover documentation to close claims efficiently and prevent later disputes.

Benefits

Why choose professional claims management.

Faster Claim Resolution

Streamlined processes and dedicated management significantly reduce claim settlement times, getting properties back to normal quicker.

Cost Control

Accurate scoping and efficient project management prevent cost overruns and ensure claims are settled comfortably within approved limits.

Quality Assurance

Professional oversight guarantees reinstatement work meets robust insurance standards and building regulations.

Single Point of Contact

A dedicated claims manager co-ordinates all parties, providing clear communication and reducing stress for policyholders.
